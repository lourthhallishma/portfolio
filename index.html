My Research Work

My academic research focuses on Machine Learning in the domain of Customer Segmentation.

Customer Segmentation Based on RFM Analysis and Unsupervised Machine Learning Technique

This research explores customer segmentation by applying Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) analysis combined with unsupervised machine learning. My findings demonstrate the effectiveness of segmenting customers into distinct behavioral groups, which can significantly improve targeted marketing strategies. This work has implications for customer relationship management and data-driven business decision-making.

Published in Advances in Data Science and Management, Springer, 2023
